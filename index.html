👋About Me

I'm an undergrad majoring in Robotics Engineering Technology at Purdue University, with a concentration in ML Optimization, dual minors in CIT and Management, and a certificate in Applications of Data Science.

I'm someone who genuinely believes the most exciting problems live at the boundary between disciplines — where machine learning meets mechanical systems, where data tells a story that changes how something moves in the real world. My goal is to drive sustainable and groundbreaking advancements in robotics, automation, and AI — I'm especially drawn to the wearable technology space and the Formula 1 racing industry, where cutting-edge engineering meets real-world performance. I want to build systems that don't just think, but act.

Beyond the technical work, I care deeply about community and giving back. I've founded two initiatives, competed at state level in basketball, hold a Taekwondo green stripe, and try to bring the same discipline I learned on the court into everything I build.
