👋About Me

I'm an undergrad majoring in Robotics Engineering Technology at Purdue University, with a concentration in AI/ML Optimization, dual minors in CIT and Management, and a certificate in Applications of Data Science.

I'm most interested in the space where machine learning meets mechanical systems — where raw data has to become a real decision, and where the right engineering call is often a strategic one too. My work spans robotics, automation, and AI, with a particular pull toward wearable technology and Formula 1, though I'm generally drawn to anything that puts engineering to work in the real world. Just as important to me as the build itself is the thinking behind it: why a solution is worth building, and who it actually serves.

Beyond the technical work, I care deeply about community and giving back. I've founded two initiatives, competed at state level in basketball, hold a Taekwondo green stripe, and try to bring the same discipline I learned on the court into everything I build.
